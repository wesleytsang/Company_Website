Speed and Efficiency

Sample: First sample will be available within 10 days, and SMS within 14-18 days. Production: Speedy production (3 times faster than usual) with punctual delivery regardless of design complexity.

Various Color and Material

Well-experienced on managing various kinds of elaborate, even fancy material. Our tight relationship with suppliers allows us to access a wide range of raw materials and available colours.

Multiple combination

Specialized skills in combining multiple knitting tension in one garment using 3-4 types of yarn in 4-5 different colours.

Flexible quantity on order

Small quantity orders from 100 pieces are welcome. For 100 pieces, maximum 2 sizes in 2 colours, a total of 4 combinations available.

Achieve your ideal design

A first sample can be provided with as much as 80% similarity to your original request based on our professional knowledge of yarn and high level of knitwear techniques. If there is unfortunately any limitation in either raw material or machine, clients will be informed before completion of the first sample.

Expert in knitwear manufacture

Full Joys is not only an expert who provides punctual, precise and high quality production of all kinds of knitwear design, but also a passionate business partner who works together with clients to bring new fashion with progressive designs to the world.
